WHY ATTEND

What you'll learn

At Config, you'll hear from the world's leading product builders on the future of design and product development. You'll also be the first to dive into Figma's newest products and features. Discover how these updates can help you stay ahead and unlock new ways of building as disciplines blur and evolve.

Who you'll meet

Over 8,000 designers, developers, marketers, and product leaders attend Config every year to share creative ideas, network, and find new ways to push the boundaries of product building. You'll have the chance to meet big-picture thinkers and everyday makers who love their craft, and connect with a community that's moving innovation forward.

What to expect

Attend inspiring keynotes from the visionaries shaping products you know and love, level up your Figma skills with hands-on demos, and dig into insightful product design topics during deep-dive sessions. You'll walk away with fresh ideas, practical takeaways, and new connections to inspire your next project.

After signing up, you'll book an appointment at one of our 2000+ partner lab locations. A quick blood draw is all that's needed. Your results will be available in the app within days, along with personalized insights and recommendations from healthcare professionals.

Yes, you'll need to visit one of our partner lab locations for a blood draw. This ensures accurate results and proper sample handling. The process is quick, typically taking less than 15 minutes, and you can choose from thousands of convenient locations nationwide.
